Brainstorming Event

Our Brainstorming Event is meant to be a low-pressure kickoff event where you can meet people, see what's in store for the semester, and get some practice coming up with design ideas. Here's how it works:

  • You sign up.
  • We send you a Zoom link.
  • On September 17 (a Thursday) at 6 PM (Central Time), you show up to the event, and everyone is overjoyed.
  • We supply some prompts, you supply ideas.
  • You meet people and decide they're the kind of folks you want to work with for the Innovation Challenge in November (see farther down this page).
  • We all feel a sense of community and inspiration that carries us through the semester, like an eagle upon a cloud, or at least a gopher on a piece of driftwood.

    • Industry Readiness Program

    The Minnovate Industry Readiness Program seeks to equip students with the skills most important and common to engineering fields, recognizing the importance of both technical and non-technical capabilities. Here's how it works:

  • Held over the course of the spring semester.
  • Groups meet once a week for 2 hours in the Anderson Student Innovation Lab.
  • Students are grouped into teams and have the entire semester to design a prototype.
  • Students receive certification from Anderson Labs on their transcripts.
  • Students build technical, teamwork, and communication skills in a hands-on project
  • Program culminates with a final presentation in front of head of Design at Anderson Labs and other program members!

    Fill out our contact form to get notified about the Industry Readiness Program!
